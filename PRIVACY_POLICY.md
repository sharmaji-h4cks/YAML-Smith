# Privacy Policy for YAML Smith

**Last Updated: January 15, 2024**

## Introduction

YAML Smith ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Cloud-Native DevOps automation platform.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Configuration Data**: When you use our generators, we process the configuration inputs you provide (e.g., application names, container images, resource specifications). **This data is processed locally in your browser and is not stored or transmitted to our servers unless explicitly submitted for generation.**

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including:
  - Generator types used (Kubernetes, Docker, Helm, CI/CD)
  - Feature usage patterns
  - Error logs (anonymized)
  - Browser type and version
  - Operating system
  - Time and date of access

- **Technical Data**:
  - IP address (anonymized)
  - Browser fingerprint (for security purposes only)
  - Session identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Service Provision**: To generate and deliver the requested configurations
2. **Service Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
3. **Security**: To detect and prevent security threats, fraud, and abuse
4. **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our platform
5. **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- We implement industry-standard security headers (HSTS, CSP, X-Frame-Options)
- Configuration data is processed transiently and not permanently stored
- We use secure coding practices to prevent XSS, CSRF, and injection attacks

### Data Retention
- **Configuration Data**: Processed in-memory and discarded immediately after generation
- **Usage Analytics**: Retained for 90 days in anonymized form
- **Error Logs**: Retained for 30 days for debugging purposes

## Cookies and Tracking

YAML Smith uses minimal cookies:
- **Essential Cookies**: Required for basic functionality (session management, security)
- **Analytics Cookies**: Optional, used to collect anonymous usage statistics (you can opt-out)

We do NOT use:
- Third-party advertising cookies
- Cross-site tracking
- User profiling for marketing purposes

## Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:
- **Analytics**: Self-hosted analytics or privacy-focused alternatives (Plausible, Matomo)
- **CDN**: For serving static assets (if applicable)
- **Error Tracking**: For monitoring application health (anonymized)

We do NOT share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request information about data we collect
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your data (if any is stored)
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics cookies at any time
- **Data Portability**: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: [privacy@yamlsmith.dev](mailto:privacy@yamlsmith.dev)

## Children's Privacy

YAML Smith is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## International Data Transfers

If you access YAML Smith from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our servers are located.
